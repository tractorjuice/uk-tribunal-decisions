Residential property tribunal decisions from England and Wales

About This Database

This database contains over 16,800 decisions from the First-tier Tribunal (Property Chamber) in England and the Residential Property Tribunal Wales. England decisions are sourced from GOV.UK and Wales decisions from residentialpropertytribunal.gov.wales.

Decision Categories

  • Leasehold Disputes — 6,492 decisions
  • Rents — 5,091 decisions
  • Park Homes — 1,816 decisions
  • Housing and Property — 1,237 decisions
  • Land Registration — 886 decisions
  • Right to Manage — 363 decisions
  • Leasehold Reform — 225 decisions

Coverage

Decisions span from 2001 to present, covering all tribunal regions across England (London, Chichester, Manchester, Birmingham, Cambridge, and Havant) and Wales. Each record includes the decision date, case reference, property address, category, and a link to the full decision.
